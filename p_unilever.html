Unilever

Supply Chain Intelligence System

Unilever is exploring the use of artificial intelligence to manage the complex task of supply chain management. I worked with the AI scientists to uncover the value that could be added into this system. Through a series of interviews and design-thinking sessions with stake holders, I created the wireframes and UI for this innovative application.


Context

I was hired as a consultant to span the roles from product development to UI design for this innovative prototype. The objective was to create a working, proof-of-concept prototype that could be used for both testing and presentation. I worked directly with AI scientists and end users.

Research Approach

After preliminary research, I realized that the original vision for the product was leaving potential value on the table. I used working sessions with the scientists and developers to create mind-maps and world cafe brainstorms to uncover a series of potential features. I conducted a battery of interviews with end users to find current pain points.

Together

Together we created valuable prototype that we could use to test with real world data and gained significant funding to move forward with our project.
